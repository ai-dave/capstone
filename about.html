Multinomial Naive Bayes classifier trained on feature vectors extracted by CountVectorizer.
Feature vectors represent 6 classes of gene families:
Gene family Number Class Label
G protein coupled receptors 531 0
Tyrosine kinase 534 1
Tyrosine phospotase 349 2
Synthetase 632 3
Synthase 711 4
Ion channel 240 5
Transcription factor 1341 6

  • Model was trained on DNA Coding Sequences from Human gene families
  • Learning generalized to Chimpanzee and Dog gene sequences
